Waffle Machine @ Vdub

Located at the Vdub Dining Hall, the waffle machines solve the problem of students wanting to eat freshly made waffles!

Interface Introduction

  1. Handle: Allows the user to hold and rotate the lid.
  2. Rotation point: Enables 180Â° rotation to ensure that waffle is heated evenly.
  3. Waffle plate: Heat the waffle mix into desired shape.
  4. Power: Connects the device to energy source.
  5. Timer: Notify the user when the waffle is ready, according to the pre-set time.

Observations

  1. People put different amount of waffle mix onto the waffle plate.
  2. For around one third of times, the liquid would spill over when the users rotate the machine.
  3. One of the users accidentally touched the waffle plate when trying to take the waffle out. He moved his hand away immediately.

Interview

A, B and C represents three different users.

  1. How often would you use a waffle machine?
    1. 5 times a week.
    2. Twice a week.
    3. Once a week.
  2. When would you use the waffle machine?
    1. Around 10am - 1pm.
    2. Around 10am - 12am.
    3. Sometime in the evening; around 4pm to6 pm.
  3. From 1 to 10, how would you rate your experience using this product?
    1. 7.
    2. 8.
    3. 7.
  4. What do you like the most about using the waffle machine?
    1. The rotation function where I can turn the plate and lid upside down.
    2. The timer.
    3. I really like how the timer makes beeping sounds when the waffle is ready.
  5. What's the hardest part about using the waffle machine?
    1. Sometimes the batter would spill over and stick on the edge of waffle plate.
    2. The waffle sticks on the lid and is difficult to get it down.
    3. Getting the waffle out from the plate.

Persona

Eric is...

A student who comes to Vdub every day for breakfast. Making a delicious warm waffle is the first thing in his daily routine.

Representing people who use the waffle machine frequently and proficient with the process.

Problem faced: The waffle takes a bit of time. When Eric wakes up late, he would get a bit rushed to wait in the line to get to use the machine, plus another 2.5 minutes for the waffle to be fully cooked.

Erica is...

A student who meets up with a friend in the evening at the Vdub, the farthest dining hall from her dorm. She did not know there are waffle machines at Vdub and decided to try it.

Representing people who use the waffle machine for their first time.

Problem faced: There are no official instructions for the first-time users. Although the operations are quite intuitive, something speaking from experience such as finding the right amount of waffle mix or taking the waffle out could be confusing for them.
